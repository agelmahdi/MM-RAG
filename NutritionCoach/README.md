# 🧠 AI Nutrition Coach

The **AI Nutrition Coach** app empowers users to make informed dietary choices using advanced AI technology.  
This app leverages the **Llama 4 Maverick 17B 128E Instruct FP8** model to analyze food images and provide valuable nutritional insights.

---

## 🚀 Key Features

### 🍽️ Food Identification and Calorie Estimation
Upload an image of your meal, and the AI model will:
- Identify the food items using visual recognition.
- Estimate the **caloric content** of each item based on a comprehensive nutritional database.

This eliminates the need for manual entry and guesswork, providing a fast and accurate overview of your meal’s calorie count.

---

### 🧾 Detailed Nutritional Breakdown
Beyond calories, the app provides a complete **nutritional profile**, including:
- Fats  
- Proteins  
- Carbohydrates  

This helps users understand the nutritional composition of their meals and align them with personal goals — whether it’s **weight loss**, **muscle gain**, or **balanced eating**.

---

### 🩺 Personalized Nutritional Advice
After analyzing your meal, the AI offers **personalized recommendations** based on:
- Your health objectives  
- The nutritional content of your meals  

For example, the app may:
- Suggest increasing fiber intake  
- Recommend adjustments to stay within target macronutrient ranges  

---

## 🧩 Technology Stack
- **Model:** Llama 4 Maverick 17B 128E Instruct FP8  
- **Core Feature:** AI-powered food image recognition and nutritional estimation
